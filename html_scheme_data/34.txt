<div class="entry-content" id="cprint">
		<p><a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka/">Karnataka government</a> is going to invite online application form for RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Admissions. Subsequently, candidates can make online registration for rte admission 2018-19 karnataka. Accordingly, all the parents who wants to get their ward (child) get admitted through 25% Reservation quota under RTE Act 2009 can fill the rte online application 2018-19. Interested candidates or their parents can fill the rte 2018-19 karnataka online application form through the official website schooleducation.kar.nic.in</p>
<div class="mobaddiv250 abc">

<p>RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Online admission process has begun. The state govt. has released / uploaded the official notification for rte admission 2018-19 karnataka on the official website on 5th February 2018. Under the Right to Education (RTE) Act 2009, the state govt. reserves 25% seats in schools for the financially poor candidates.</p>
<p><b>Latest Update (28 February 2018)</b> &#8211; Department of Primary Education has released a circular about the admission process in Aided Schools under RTE (1)(b) for the year 2018-19 to Publish School-Wise seats in the software. To get details about the new circular, please &#8211; <a href="http://schooleducation.kar.nic.in/pdffiles/RTE%20admission21022018.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Click Here</a> </p>

<p>Candidates can now know their school to apply for RTE Karnataka Admissions 2018-19. Interested and eligible candidates can apply for rte admission 2018-19 karnataka through filling the rte online application 2018-19 before the closure date.</p>

<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="rte-karnataka-201819-online-application-/-rte-karnataka-201819-admission"></span>RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Online Application / RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Admission</h2>
<p>The complete procedure for to apply for RTE Admission 2018-19 Karnataka is as follows:-</p>

<li>Subsequently on the homepage, go the Administration section present at the bottom.</li>
<li>Click at the &#8220;RTE Admissions&#8221; tab under &#8216;Right to Education&#8217; section present on the left side.</li>
<li>Next rte online application 2018-19 link will get updated at the official website.</li>

<p>RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Online Application Form Date is not validated till now. Under Right To Education Act &#8211; RTE 2009, Karnataka govt. gives direction to every school to reserve some seats in various government as well as private schools. Accordingly, govt. reserve these seats for the students having a poor financial background. Moreover, the RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Admission Online Application for the new academic year will begin very soon.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="rte-karnataka-admissions-software-%e2%80%93-original-version"></span>RTE Karnataka Admissions Software &#8211; Original Version</h3>
<p>Department of Public instruction, Karnataka has released the original version of the RTE 2018 Admission Software. Candidates can fill the RTE Karnataka 2018-19 Application Form for Students through the link given below:-<br/>
http://164.100.133.126/RTE2018/(S(e35fkzxiioolfcphmvg1j4ql))/RTE2017/RTE2018_admit.aspx<br/>
Here candidates have to enter their Aadhaar ID and then authenticate using the OTP or Fingerprint method. Finally, candidates can submit it to complete RTE Application Submission Process. </p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="rte-admission-201819-karnataka-%e2%80%93-school-login"></span>RTE Admission 2018-19 Karnataka &#8211; School Login</h3>
<p>Below is the complete procedure to apply for RTE School Login 2018:-</p>
<ol>
<li>Schools authorities can firstly visit the official website schooleducation.kar.nic.in</li>
<li>Subsequently, go to &#8220;Links to Department of Software&#8221; section present at the right side of the page.</li>
<li>Accordingly click the &#8216;RTE School Login 2018&#8217; link</li>

a) Income Certificate<br/>

c) Domicile / Residential Certificate</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="age-limit-to-fill-rte-201819-karnataka-online-application-form"></span>Age Limit to fill RTE 2018-19 Karnataka Online Application Form</h3>
<p>The age limit as per 2017 admission rules is described below:-</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Pre School / LKG Admissions</b> &#8211; The age of children must lie between 3 years 10 months and 4 years 10 months.</li>
<li><b>1st Standard Admission</b> &#8211; The age of the candidate must lie between 5 years 10 months to 6 years 10 months (as on 1st June of the Academic Year) to get admitted in 1st standard.</li>
</ul>
