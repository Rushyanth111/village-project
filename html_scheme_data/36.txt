		<p><a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka/">Karnataka</a> Examinations Authority (KEA) is inviting online applications for Karnataka CET 2018 (KCET) Examination. Subsequently, KCET exam is for admission in various undergraduate as well as professional courses in different colleges of Karnataka. Accordingly, interested candidates can apply online for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test 2018 through the official website kea.kar.nic.in</p>

<p>KCET exam is essential to get admissions in Medical, Dental, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani, Yoga, Engineering, Naturopathy and Architecture courses in govt. / govt. aided / private institutions.</p>
<p>Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website before 26 February 2018.</p>

<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="karnataka-cet-2018-online-application-procedure"></span>Karnataka CET 2018 Online Application Procedure</h2>
<p>The complete procedure to apply online has been specified below:-</p> 
<ul>
<li>Firstly visit the official website <a href="http://kea.kar.nic.in/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">kea.kar.nic.in</a></li>
<li>Subsequently on the homepage, click the &#8220;CET 2018&#8221; link present on the right side of the page.</li>
<li>Then in the new window, click the &#8220;CET 2018 &#8211; UG Online Application&#8221; link or directly click this link</li>
<li>Afterwards on the same page, click the &#8220;ಹೊಸ ಬಳಕೆದಾರ ? ಇಲ್ಲಿ ಕ್ಲಿಕ್ ಮಾಡಿ ನೋಂದಣಿ &#8211; New User ? Click Here To Register&#8221; link</li>
<li>Read the instructions carefully and click the &#8220;Proceed to Apply Online&#8221; link and then the Registration Form will appear as shown below:-</li>
<div id="attachment_8122" style="width: 910px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8122" src="https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details.jpg" alt="Karnataka CET 2018 KCET Registration Form" width="900" height="352" class="size-full wp-image-8122 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-srcset="https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details.jpg 768w, https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details-300x117.jpg 300w" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px" data-lazy-src="https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details.jpg?is-pending-load=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7"><noscript><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8122" src="https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details.jpg" alt="Karnataka CET 2018 KCET Registration Form" width="900" height="352" class="size-full wp-image-8122" srcset="https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details.jpg 768w, https://sarkariyojana.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/karnataka-cet-2018-kcet-application-form-exam-date-details-300x117.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px"/></noscript><p id="caption-attachment-8122" class="wp-caption-text">Karnataka CET 2018 KCET Registration Form</p></div>
<li>Here candidates have to enter necessary details and click the submit button to proceed to fill the UG Online Application Form.</li>
<li>Finally, candidates can click the &#8220;Submit&#8221; button to complete the application process.</li>
</ul>
<p>Furthermore, candidates can take a printout of the completed application form and keep it for any future reference.</p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="important-dates-/-exam-date"></span>Important Dates / Exam Date</h3>
<p>Important date to fill online application form are as follows:-</p>
<table width="100%">
<tr>
<td>Starting Date of Online Applications</td>
<td>1 February 2018</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Last Date to Fill Online Application</td>
<td>26 February 2018</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>End Date to Pay Application Fees</td>
<td>28 February 2018</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Downloading of Admit Cards</td>
<td>10 April 2018</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>KCET 2018 Exam Date</td>
<td>18 April 2018 (Biology, Mathematics) &#038; 19 April 2018 (Physics, Chemistry)</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Kannada Language Test (For Horanadu &#038; Gadinadu Kannadiga Candidates)</td>
<td>20 April 2018</td>
</tr>
</table>
