		<p><a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka/">Karnataka govt.</a> is inviting online applications for Chief Minister Self Employment Scheme at cmegp.kar.nic.in to provide govt. subsidy on loans to individual entrepreneurs in rural areas. Each beneficiary can avail subsidy ranging from 25% to 35% on a single project costing upto Rs. 10 lakh. Interested candidates can check CMEGP details, documents before filling cm self employment scheme online application form 2019 at the official website. </p>

<p>The state government of Karnataka provides government loans to individual entrepreneurs to generate self employment opportunities under chief minister&#8217;s self employment generation programme. Beneficiaries can start a new project by making own contribution of just 5% (special category) and 10% (general category) of the total project cost.</p>
<p>Department of Commerce, Industry and Tourism is the nodal agency for implementation of CMEGP scheme in Karnataka. Candidates can apply through either online or offline mode to avail scheme benefits.</p>

<tr>
<td>Recent passport photograph</td>
<td>Detailed project report (DPR)</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Age proof</td>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="cmegp-details-eligibility-criteria"></span>CMEGP Details &#038; Eligibility Criteria</h3>
<p>All the applicants can check the CMEGP details and eligibility criteria before filling the CMEGP online application form:-</p>
<ul>
<li>Individual entrepreneurs in rural areas can avail margin money or govt. subsidy ranging from 25% to 35% of total project cost.</li>
<li>Under CMEGP, the maximum allowed project cost is Rs. 10 lakh for any single unit.</li>
<li>Availability of the capital expenditure loan will be only or manufacturing and service activities only.</li>
<li>All general category entrepreneurs can avail subsidy upto Rs. 2.5 lakh while entrepreneurs belonging to SC /ST / OBC / PH / Women / Minorities category can avail subsidy upto Rs. 3.5 lakh.</li>
<li>Pubic sector banks and Regional rural banks (RRB) are the financial agencies to provide margin money to entrepreneurs.</li>
<li>The entrepreneurs must fulfill the age criteria to become eligible for CMEGP. For general category candidates, the age must lie between 21 to 35 years while for special category candidates, the specified age group is 21 to 45 years.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurship Development Training (EDP) to the beneficiary is compulsory at CEDOK &#8211; Centre for Entrepreneurship Development of Karnataka or at RUDSET &#8211; Rural Development &#038; Self Employment Training.</li>
<li>Only one unit can be setup by a family to get assistance under CMEGP in order to provide benefits to a large number of families.</li>
<li>The govt. subsidy is available only for a single new unit which is setup in rural area with a lock-in period of 3 years and even there is no income limit.</li>
<li>General category beneficiaries needs to contribute 10% of the total project cost while the special category beneficiary entrepreneurs needs to contribute 5% of the project cost.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="chief-minister-self-employment-scheme-helpline-numbers"></span>Chief Minister Self Employment Scheme Helpline Numbers</h3>
<p>In case of any query regarding CMEGP application process, applicants can contact the Head office / Corporate Office at &#8211; Department of Industries and Commerce, #49, South Block, Khanija Bhavan, Race Course Road, Bengaluru &#8211; 560001.</p>
