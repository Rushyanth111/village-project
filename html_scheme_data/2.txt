In the Karnataka Jana Sevaka Yojana, you can book your slot via call center (080-44554455) or mobile app or website (janasevaka.karnataka.gov.in). People can even check the complete list of 53 services of various departments which are available for home delivery. The procedure to request a service is now available at the official website, so be fast and order service you want to be home delivered.</p>

Here we are providing you with the complete schemes / services list under Jana Sevaka program. </p>
<p>Moreover, the Janasevaka service request procedure to book your slot is hereby mentioned. Now, people will not have to make unnecessary visits at the government offices and stand in queues. Rather they can just simply place a request and get home delivery of services in a hassle free manner.   </p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="karnataka-janasevaka-service-procedure-to-book-your-slot-for-via-call-center"></span>Karnataka Janasevaka Service Procedure to Book Your Slot for via Call Center</h3>
<p>Below are the steps to be followed to place a Karnataka Janasevaka Service request to book your slot via call center:-</p>
<div class="googleads">
<center>
<script type="text/javascript">google_ad_client="ca-pub-3737030722290655";google_ad_slot="CM_Sarkariyojana_BTF_WC3";google_ad_width=336;google_ad_height=280;</script>
<script type="text/javascript" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js"></script>
</center>
</div>

<ul>
<li><b>Step 1</b> &#8211; Firstly, citizen can place a call at the call center till free number 08044554455 and request for a service.</li>
<li><b>Step 2</b> &#8211; The call center executive who attends the call will explain the details of the service such as documents required, service fee and other related things.</li>
<li><b>Step 3</b> &#8211; Upon receiving confirmation from citizen, desired slot will be booked on the basis of availability. An OTP would be sent on registered mobile number of citizen. This OTP is required to be shared with Jana Sevaka at time of service delivery.</li>
<li><b>Step 4</b> &#8211; After booking confirmation, Jana Sevaka will be assigned to fulfill the service at that particular slot. Jana Sevaka will visit the citizen&#8217;s home on the requested date and time. After sharing of OTP with Jana Sevaka, he/she will assist the citizen in filling the service application form. He/she will also scan and upload any document required to avail the service.</li>
<li><b>Step 5</b> &#8211; Accordingly, Jana Sevaka will collect service fee along with department fee required to avail the service.</li>
<li><b>Step 6</b> &#8211; Afterwards, citizen will get an acknowledgment via SMS on their registered mobile number.</li>
<li><b>Step 7</b> &#8211; Once the application is processed by the concerned department, Jana Sevaka will deliver the certificate/NOC/Permission/License etc to citizen&#8217;s home.</li>
<p>After service is completed, feedback will be sought from citizens for further improvement of service delivery under Karnataka Janasevaka Scheme 2020.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="karnataka-janasevaka-service-procedure-to-book-slot-via-mobile-app"></span>Karnataka Janasevaka Service Procedure to Book Slot via Mobile App</h3>
<p>Here are the steps of Karnataka Janasevaka placing service request to book slot via mobile app:-</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Step 1</b> &#8211; Go to Android Play Store or Apple Store and download Karnataka Janasevaka Mobile One App using the link:-<br/>
<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.imi.karnatakamobileone" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.imi.karnatakamobileone</a></li>
<li><b>Step 2</b> &#8211; Log on to the Karnataka Janasevaka mobile app and click on Jana Sevaka. Subsequently, citizens can check the list of available services on the app. Upon clicking the service required, service details (documents required, service fee etc) would be displayed.</li>
<li><b>Step 3</b> &#8211; After confirmation from the citizens, available slots would be displayed to citizens. Citizen can book any of the available slot based on their convenience. Once the slot is booked, an OTP will be sent to citizen which has to be shared with Jana Sevaka at time of service delivery.</li>
<li><b>Step 4 to 7</b> &#8211; All the steps from Step 4 to 7 are same as mentioned above.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="karnataka-janasevaka-service-procedure-to-book-your-slot-for-via-website"></span>Karnataka Janasevaka Service Procedure to Book Your Slot for via Website</h3>
<p>All the citizens can even place Karnataka Janasevaka service request through the official website. The link to access official website is <a href="http://www.janasevaka.karnataka.gov.in/index.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">janasevaka.karnataka.gov.in</a>. The direct link to book slot to avail services at home is given below:-<br/>
<a href="http://www.janasevaka.karnataka.gov.in/bookslot.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://www.janasevaka.karnataka.gov.in/bookslot.html</a></p>
