<div class="entry-content" id="cprint">
		<p>Karnataka Minority Development Corporation Ltd. is now inviting online application form for Ganga Kalyana Scheme 2018. Under this scheme, all the beneficiaries will get one drilled borewell / open well with pump set from KDMC or lift irrigation facility. The beneficiaries in the selection list must belong to the minority community and must be a small / marginal farmer. Candidates can download the online application form through kmdc.karnataka.gov.in  </p>

<p><a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka/">Karnataka</a> Farmers will get appropriate irrigation facilities through utilization of perennial sources of water or lifting water through pipelines. In case the perennial water sources are not available then KDMC will provide loans for individual borewell construction on water points recommended by expert geologists.</p>
<p>KDMC will bear the total expenditure of Rs. 1.5 lakh for construction of borewells to promote the agricultural activities.</p>

<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="lift-irrigation-scheme-%e2%80%93-eligibility-for-ganga-kalyan-scheme-2018"></span>Lift Irrigation Scheme &#8211; Eligibility for Ganga Kalyan Scheme 2018</h3>
<p>This type of irrigation facility is given to the agricultural land through efficient utilization of perennial water sources like rivers. To provide adequate water supply for irrigation, water if lifted through pipe lines. The candidates applying for Lift Irrigation facility must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to avail benefits under Ganga Kalyana Yojana 2018:-  </p>
<ol>
<li>All the beneficiaries must belong to the minority community as per the <a href="http://kmdc.karnataka.gov.in/assets/downloads/govt_order_gky_n.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">government order</a>.</li>
<li>Applicant must be a permanent resident of Karnataka.</li>
<li>Candidates must be a small or marginal farmer.</li>
<li>The annual family income of the farmers from all sources must not exceed Rs. 22,000 p.a</li>
</ol>
<p>Farmers having less than 5 acres of land in addition to the precondition that the land of 7 minority persons is adjacent can avail Lift Irrigation Scheme benefits under Ganga Kalyana Scheme 2018.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="individual-borewell-%e2%80%93-eligibility-for-ganaga-kalyana-scheme-2018"></span>Individual Borewell &#8211; Eligibility for Ganaga Kalyana Scheme 2018</h3>
<p>In case of non-availability of perennial water sources to irrigate land, KDMC provides subsidy for individual borewells construction. Expert geologists identify water points inside the ground appropriate for construction of borewell. KDMC then maintains these borewells for 5 years and then transfers them to consumer co-operatives of beneficaries.</p>
<p>For farmers having land holding between 2 to 5 acres, KDMC will drill a single borewell / open well and supply pump sets. This scheme cost Rs. 1,50,000 per beneficiary which also includes the pump and energization cost. The eligibility criteria is same as mentioned above.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="ganga-kalyana-borewell-selection-list-and-procedure"></span>Ganga Kalyana Borewell Selection List and Procedure</h3>
<p>In each district, district managers invites application through advertisement in newspapers. Afterwards, District Manager will screen the received applicants and forward it to the Taluk Committee headed by MLA. Afterwards, committee will forward the proposal to the concerned department.</p>
