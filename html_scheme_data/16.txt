<div class="entry-content" id="cprint">
		<p>Karnataka Farm Loan Waiver List is available at the official website clws.karnataka.gov.in. Now any farmer can find their name in the Crop Loan Waiver Scheme (CLWS) list of beneficiaries 2019 released by the <a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka/">Karnataka government</a>. The complete payment and loan status report is available and can be checked using Aadhaar number and Ration Card number. The report would contain the commercial bank loan details, bank payment details, Primary Agricultural Cooperative Society (PACS) loan details, PACS Payment details and can be downloaded / printed.  </p>
<div class="mobaddiv250 abc">

<p>Crop Loan Waiver individual loanee report, citizen payment certificate for PACS and citizen payment certificate for banks is available at the official website. All the beneficiary farmers can check the Crop Loan Waiver Certificate for citizen using aadhaar card number, ration card number and FSD ID.</p>
<p>The complete list of beneficiaries of <a href="https://sarkariyojana.com/karnataka-crop-farm-loan-waiver-scheme-farmers/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Karnataka Farmer Loan Waiver Scheme</a> who have taken loans from cooperative sector or commercial sector is now available.</p>

<h2>Karnataka Farm Loan Waiver List 2019</h2>
<p>Below is the complete procedure to check the name of beneficiary farmers in the Karnataka Farm Loan Waiver List:-</p> 
<ul>
<li>Firstly visit the official website <a href="http://clws.karnataka.gov.in/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">clws.karnataka.gov.in</a></li>
<li>At the homepage, go to the &#8220;<b>Services for Citizen</b>&#8221; section at the official website as shown below:-</li>
